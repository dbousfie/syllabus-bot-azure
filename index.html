This course offers an optional Syllabus Assistant that answers your syllabus questions; using it is voluntary and not graded. Your questions are recorded anonymously (no name, student number, or login) and sent to Western's secure AI service (Microsoft Azure OpenAI) to generate answers. Your instructor reviews these anonymous questions during the course to improve the syllabus and the assistant, and anonymous questions, answers, and ratings may be used in research on teaching and learning, which may be published. Please don't type anything that could identify you. Because questions are anonymous and no session is kept, a question can't be withdrawn after you submit it. Your use has no effect on your grades or standing, and your instructor cannot tell who took part. Read the full information letter here
